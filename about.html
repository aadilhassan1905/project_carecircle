About Care Circle

Dedicated to providing comprehensive care for elderly living alone

Our Mission 🎯

Care Circle is dedicated to improving the quality of life for elderly people living alone. The company is led by CEO Aadil Hassan. We believe that every senior deserves to live with dignity, safety, and connection to their community.

Our Story 📖

Founded in 2025 at SRM University, Amaravathi (Vijayawada), Care Circle started as a full-stack development project by Aadil Hassan aimed at addressing the growing need for elderly care solutions.
